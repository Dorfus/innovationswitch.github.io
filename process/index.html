Innovation Process

The 5 steps to a succesfull lean startup

1. Strategic Alignment

Gear the innovation process for ROI by aligning to business strategy. Assign targets and set budgets for innovation.

2. Leadership Commitment

Executives set innovation challenges, creating buy-in. Innovation champions are identified within the organisation to build momentum & fast-track.

3. Financial Backing

CFO verifies business cases & ROI. Pledge funds to innovations. Select which innovations to invest in.

4. Build & Track MVP

Apply lean start-up methodology, adapted for corporates. Quickly test, proceed, pivot or end an innovation.

5. Measure & Monitor ROI

Line-of-sight dashboards track & predict ROI. Use dashboards to drive behaviour. Collate results from innovations and verify ROI.
